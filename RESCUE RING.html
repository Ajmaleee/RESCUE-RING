Welcome to Rescue Ring

Rescue Ring is a revolutionary geofencing safety device designed to prevent individuals from entering unsafe areas near water bodies. Using advanced GPS and GSM technologies, Rescue Ring provides real-time alerts and notifications to both users and emergency contacts, ensuring safety and quick response in critical situations.

Features

  • Real-Time GPS Location Tracking
  • Automatic Calls to Emergency Contacts
  • Geofencing to Define Safe and Unsafe Zones
  • Portable and Easy to Use
  • Durable and Water-Resistant Design
  • Customizable Alert Frequency and Boundaries

How It Works

Rescue Ring utilizes GPS to track the wearer's location in real-time. When a user approaches or crosses a predefined boundary, the system immediately alerts emergency contacts via SMS. If the boundary is crossed, the device will initiate a call to the emergency contact, ensuring prompt assistance.
